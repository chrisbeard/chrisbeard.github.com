Music

I've been playing trombone since 2003, and I currently study with Don Robertson at Binghamton University.

I am a member of Don's low brass studio ensemble, as well as the Binghamton University Symphony Orchestra, which is directed by Dr. Timothy Perry.

You might also see me play in some of the music department's recitals, or at Binghamton's commencement ceremonies.

I make multi-track recordings of myself on trombone, and compose alternative electronic music. Some of my work is available on soundcloud.

Programming
